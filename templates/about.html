The Founders

Born in 2001, after having studied together at UPRM, Yariel Mercado and Xavier Rosado had a vision: to establish a cafe worthy of providing the pinnacle of fresh food. Together, Yariel, with an affinity for pastries, and Xavier, an avid coffee connoisseur, established a Franco-Italian utopia for the craft.

The University of Puerto Rico at Mayagüez is the alma matter of these rising entrepreneurs.

Humble Beginnings

Starting in the humble beginning of their homes, later an affordable yet homey locale, and finally our current location in Washington, Yariel and Xavier have always believed in using the freshest of ingredients in what they serve. While the locales may change, our philosphy and crème of the crop ingredients doesn't.

All our ingredients are either locally sourced or imported directly from the freshest countrysides in Europe.

Art Through Food

Creating food is an art, and we strongly uphold the craft by proving that its more than following cookbooks. It's about the technique, quality of the ingredients, and love put into it. From fresh vanilla beans from the Madagascan country side to the care invested into the delicate process of making buttery and flakey croissants, we uphold our mission to deliver the finest European pastries in the west.

"When you acknowledge, as you must, that there is no such thing as perfect food... then the real purpose of striving toward perfection becomes clear: to make people happy, that is what cooking is all about." ~Thomas Keller
